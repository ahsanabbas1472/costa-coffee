Our Sustainability Efforts

At Costa Coffee, we’re just as committed to
our communities, the people we serve and the
world we live in as we are about serving great
coffee. We are always working on crafting
more sustainable coffee – from bean to cup.

Our Story

It began in 1971 when two brothers, Sergio and
Bruno Costa, moved to London with the desire
to make great coffee. They went on to blind-
test 112 different variations of coffee before
arriving at the perfect one, the ‘Mocha Italia
’, which remains our signature blend to this day.
